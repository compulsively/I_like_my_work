# Audit Rubric

## Severity

| Level | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Blocker | Prevents review, use, development, testing, or decision. |
| Major | Likely causes misunderstanding, rework, or wrong output. |
| Minor | Improves clarity, completeness, or polish. |
| Note | Optional suggestion or residual risk. |

## Core Checks

1. Does it answer the original request?
2. Can the target reader understand what to do next?
3. Are input, output, scope, and non-scope clear?
4. Are key flows, states, and exceptions covered when needed?
5. Are acceptance or success criteria visible?
6. Are assumptions separated from facts?
7. Is there any obvious contradiction?
8. Is the next fix concrete?

## Not Covered

This audit does not replace:

- legal review
- medical review
- security review
- finance review
- formal compliance approval
- production readiness sign-off
