# General Audit Lite

## Purpose

Review whether an artifact is good enough for its intended next step and identify the smallest useful fixes.

## Load Order

1. Read `references/audit-rubric.md`.
2. Read `references/audit-output-template.md`.

## Workflow

1. Identify the artifact type and target reader.
2. Restate the expected goal in one sentence.
3. Check whether the artifact meets the goal.
4. Find blocking or high-impact gaps first.
5. Give specific fixes, not vague quality comments.
6. Mark unknown facts and unsupported assumptions.

## Output Rules

- Findings first.
- Be concise and practical.
- Do not rewrite the whole artifact unless asked.
- Do not pretend to perform a specialized compliance review.
- If the artifact is already good enough, say so and mention remaining minor risks.

## Default Output Shape

```markdown
## Audit Conclusion

## Blocking Issues

## Important Improvements

## Minor Suggestions

## Suggested Fixes

## Open Questions
```
