## Prototype Review Add-On

For prototypes, also check:

- Is the first screen meaningful?
- Is the next action obvious?
- Are interactions clickable or clearly described?
- Does the prototype show result states?
- Are internal notes hidden from delivery view if needed?

## Backend Workflow Add-On

For backend or admin workflows, also check:

- Can operators scan key records quickly?
- Are filters and status tags useful?
- Are batch actions safe and recoverable?
- Is permission or failure feedback clear?
- Are logs or operation results visible when needed?
